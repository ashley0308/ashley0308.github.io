About Us

Laura Smith has loved animals her whole life. She grew up on her family's farm, where she helped care for all kinds of pets and even took in strays that needed a home. Because of that passion, she decided to start her own pet sitting business called Paws Sitter. Laura runs it with the help of two part time assistants who are trained in pet safety and first aid, so clients know their pets are in good care.
